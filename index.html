About

History

Plotify is a student project initiated as a school assignment. Five students work together on a project for one semester, coached by faculty staff.

Concept

Plotify is a CNC plotting printer, drawing inputs are presented by the computer. Sensors are being used to position the pen at the starting location. The main mechanism works like a CNC gantry router.

Design

Design is important and fun!

Realisation

Plottify is constructed using mainly Fisher-Technik®, we use other construction parts to get a coherent design.
